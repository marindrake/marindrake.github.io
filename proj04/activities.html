Adventure trip / Activities

Whitewater rafting

Full Day Trip | Class III (IV) | Adult - $119/Youth - $89

1/2 Day Trip | Class III (IV) | Adult - $89/Youth - $71

The Salmon River provides an ideal experience for first-timers or else those looking for the more mellow experience. Your guide can take alternate, more easygoing lines, or depending on the time of year, we may choose one of the many other sections of the Salmon to float. Regardless, this whitewater trip is great for any and all, the thrill-tempered-traveler and the swashbuckling Braveheart.

On our trip you can choose to ride in a raft, paddle in a raft, or paddle an inflatable kayak for those looking for the bigger adventure. The choice is up to you!

Kayaking

Full 6-Day Trip | Class III (IV) | Adult - $999/Youth - $789

We offer guided inflatable kayaking trips, single, half, and multi-day inflatable kayaking adventures on the Salmon River. On these unique trips, you get the chance to paddle yourself through the throws and thrills of the Salmon River's rapids and to float stretched-out and feet-up in the its calm pools.

Guides paddling their own inflatable kayaks will help teach you the basics and then lead you "follow-the-leader" style through the rapids. If you should fall out: no problem; the guides will be right there to pick you up. It's all part of the fun. So, the question is: are you a "do-it-yourselfer"? Looking for a little more adventure? Well here is your chance to sit above the rapids, hear their roar, and power your way down through the waves on your own.

Fishing Trips

Full Day Trip | All levels | $379 per boat

1/2 Day Trip | All levels | $219 per boat

The Salmon River is one of the country's most famous fisheries, including Chinook Salmon and Steelhead Trout. All of our Steelhead Fishing Trips set out on heated, dory-style drift boats, and are led by our truly peerless guides.

Whether a first-timer or master-angler, fly or spin fisher, we have the experience and expert guidance, not to mention the custom driftboat, to find you the fish you're looking for. All trips include: heated drift boats, guide, shuttles, homemade snacks, hot and cold drinks, all equipment, and a delicious riverside lunch.
